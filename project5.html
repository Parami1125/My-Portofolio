Unveiling Consumer Trends: A Multi-Algorithm Analysis of Nike Shoe Sales Using SAS and CRISP-DM

This blog delves into the analysis of Nike shoe sales, focusing on consumer purchasing patterns and product preferences. By employing advanced data analysis techniques such as SAS and the CRISP-DM methodology, we aim to uncover insights that can drive better product design and marketing strategies for Nike and similar brands.

Key Insights from Data Analysis

Utilizing statistical analysis and machine learning algorithms like Decision Trees, Support Vector Machines (SVM), and Logistic Regression, we aim to reveal trends in consumer behavior regarding Nike shoes. This analysis highlights the most popular products, valued features, and factors influencing consumer choices, thus providing actionable insights for Nike's marketing and product strategies.

Analyzing Consumer Preferences with CRISP-DM

Through the CRISP-DM framework, we systematically analyze the sales dataset to identify patterns and preferences among consumers. By understanding the intricacies of consumer behavior, businesses can tailor their offerings and improve customer engagement, ultimately driving increased sales and market share for Nike and other brands in the athletic footwear market.
